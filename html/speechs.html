Speech

Speech :- Parts of speech are a very important part of English grammar. For an ESL student learning parts of speech is a must thing to do. It will help in building the initial concepts. It is the building block of English grammar.

⇒ Here is the quick list of 8 Parts of Speech:

1. Noun / ስም 2. Pronoun / ተውላጠ ስም 3. Adjective / ቅፅል 4. Verb / ግሲ 5. Adverb / ተውሳኸ ግሲ 6. Preposition / መስተዋድድ 7. Conjunction / መስተጻምር 8. Interjection / ቃለ-ኣጋንኖ


1. Noun / ስም

Noun :- is known as naming words that we use for giving a name to people, places, things and ideas. Like Semir, English or Lion etc.
⇒ ስም ማለት ንሰባት ፣ ንቦታታት ፣ ንነገራትን ንሓሳባትን መፀውዒ ንምሃብ ንጥቀመሉ ቃል እዩ።

⇒ There are further two major types of nouns Common nouns and Proper nouns.
⇒ Common noun: The name of a common place, non-specific person or a thing is known as a common noun like chair, book, city, etc.
⇒ Proper noun: The name of a special place, person, or thing is known as a proper noun like Muhammad, Daniel, Liwam, Amanuel, etc.

Example: An old woman brought me a very long dress from a tailor, and then she quickly disappeared.

2. Pronoun / ተውላጠ-ስም

Pronoun :- is a word that is used instead of (at the place of) a noun. Such as he, she and it etc.
⇒ ተውላጠ ስም ከክንዲ ስም ተኪኡ ዝኣቱ ቃል እዩ።

⇒ Basically, the pronoun is a word that is usually substituted for a noun.
⇒ Pronouns are further defined by their type such as:
⇒ Personal pronoun: refers to a specific person or a thing he, she, it etc.
⇒ Reflexive pronoun: is used to emphasise another noun or a pronoun myself, himself etc.
⇒ Possessive pronoun: indicate ownership of his, her, its etc.

Example: An old woman brought me a very long dress from a tailor, and then she quickly disappeared.

3. Adjective / ቅፅል

Adjective :- is word which qualifies or modifies a noun or pronoun is known as an adjective such as sweet, pretty, hot etc.
⇒ ቅፅል ን ስም ወይ ተውላጠ ስም ዝገልፅ (ዘብራህርህ) ቃል እዩ።

⇒ It can also specify the size, quality, and a number of things.

Example: An old woman brought me a very long dress from a tailor, and then she quickly disappeared.

4. Verb / ግሲ

Verb :- is a word that expresses an action and state. Such asbeing, doing, or having.
⇒ ግሲ ተግባርን ኲነትን ዝገልፅ ቃል እዩ።

⇒ There is a main verb in a sentence and sometimes one or more helping verbs.
→ Such as ( she can sing. Here sing is the main verb and can be a helping verb).

Example: An old woman brought me a very long dress from a tailor, and then she quickly disappeared.

5. Adverb / ተውሳኸ ግሲ

Adverb :- is a word that modifies a verb, an adverb, an adjective, another adverb or a preposition.
⇒ ተውሳኸ ግሲ ማለት ግሲ ፣ ቅፅል ፣ ካሊእ ተውሳኸ ግሲን ወይ መስተዋድድን ዝገልፅ ቃል እዩ። >

⇒ They are usually used to answer the question of when, where, how (it gives more information about time, place and manner). They usually end with the word –ly.
⇒ ተውሳኸ ግሲ ብዛዕባ ሓደ ፍፃመ/ኲነት መዓዝ ፣ ኣበይን ከመይን ከም ዝተፈፀመ ሓበሬታ ይህብ።

Example: An old woman brought me a very long dress from a tailor, and then she quickly disappeared.

6. Preposition / መስተዋድድ

Preposition :- is a that describes relationships of words in a sentence. Such as by, with, on etc.
⇒ መስተዋድድ ኣብ ምሉእ ሓሳብ ዉሽጢ ናይ ቃላት ዝምድና/ እስስር ዝገልፅ ቃል እዩ።

Example:
→The book is on the table.
→He wrote a letter with the blue pen.

7. Conjunction / መስተጻምር

Conjunction :- is a word used to join words, clauses, phrases or sentences and indicates the relationship between them, such as but, or so, yet are conjunctions.
⇒ መስተጻምር ቃላት ፣ ሓረጋትን ምሉእ ሓሳባትን ንምትሕሓዝ/ ንምርኻብ ዘገልግል ቃል እዩ። ከምኡ'ውን ኣብ ሞንጎኦም ዘሎ ዝምድና ይሕብር።

8. Interjection / ቃለ-ኣጋንኖ

Interjection :- can be a short sound, word or phrase spoken suddenly to expresse an emotion/ feeling. It stands alone and ends with an exclamation mark (!). Such as Oo!, Woo!, Alas! etc.
⇒ ቃለ-ኣጋንኖ ስምዒት ንምግላፅ ብሃንደበት ዝንገር ሓፂር ድምፂ ፣ ቃል ወይ ድማ ሓረግ ክኸውን ይኽእል። ቃለ ኣጋንኖ በይኑ ደው ዝብልን ብምልክት ትእምርተ-ኣንክሮ (!) ዝውድእ እዩ።

⇒ It is often followed by the sign of exclamation(!).

Example:
→ Oh my!
→ Hurrah! We won the match
