Tenses

Tense :- comes from the Latin word "Tempus" which means time.

⇒ It is a word that expresses the time of an action.

⇒ There are three main types of tenses in English. There are:-

1. Present Tense / ህልዊ እዋን 2. Past Tense / ሕሉፍ እዋን 3. Future Tense / መፃኢ እዋን
