Idioms

Idiom :- comes from the Greek word "Idios" which means for one's own or private.

⇒ It is a group of words whose meanings is different from the meaning of the individual words in it.

⇒ When we use it in the right way and the right place it becomes a powerful linguistic stool.

Examples:

  1. On the top of the world = Extremly happy
    ⇒ ካብ መጠን ንላዕሊ ሕጉስ
  2. A piece of cake = Simple
    ⇒ ቀሊል
  3. Crying for the moon = Asking difficult request
    ⇒ ከቢድ ዝኾነ ሕቶ ምሕታት
  4. At the eleventh hour (11thhr) = At the last moment (time)
    ⇒ ኣብ መወዳእታ ሰዓት
  5. At sea = Confused
    ⇒ ምድንጋር
  6. Hit the books = Study hard
    ⇒ ጠንኪርካ ኣፅንዕ
  7. Turn a deaf ear = Ignore what someone is saying
    ⇒ ፀማም እዝኒ ሂቡኒ
  8. Kill the birds with a stone = Achieve two results by doing one thing
    ⇒ ብሓደ እምኒ ክልተ ኣዕዋፍ
  9. Every dog has it's day = Every one get chance
    ⇒ ኩሉ ሰብ ዕድል ክረክብ ኣለዎ / ኩሉ 'ኮ ግዜ ኣለዎ
  10. Time is money = To work quickly in the time left
    ⇒ እታ ዘላ ክትጥቀመላ ኣለካ
  11. It is always darkest before dawn = Something good is coming
    ⇒ ድሕሪ ፀላም ብርሃን ኣሎ
  12. Apple of my eye = I like him / her
    ⇒ ብሌን ዓይነይ / ይፈትዎ/ዋ
  13. I'm under the weather = Feeling ill (sick)
    ⇒ ሓሚመ / ይሒሙኒ
  14. Getting cold feet = Nervous
    ⇒ ተናደደ / ተቖጠዐ
  15. Run out of = Finish the supply / Used up
    ⇒ ተወዲኡ / ተጨሊጡ
  16. Pick up = Obtain / get / bring
    ⇒ ምምፃእ (ንዝኾነ ነገር)
  17. Odds and ends = Miscellaneous items (goods)
    ⇒ ዝተፈላለዩ ነገራት
  18. Fed up with = Disgusted with / Had enough of
    ⇒ ቅጭ ምባል / ምፅላእ / ምስልቻው
  19. Foot the bill = Pay
    ⇒ ምኽፋል (ከፈለ)
  20. Down the drain (tube) = Lost / wasted
    ⇒ ምጥፋእ / ምብኻን
  21. Chin in = Contribute / give jointly
    ⇒ ኣስተዋፅኦ (ወፈያ) ምግባር
  22. Skip = Forget / pass over
    ⇒ ምግዳፍ / ምሕላፍ / ምዝላል
  23. Caught short = Having an insufficient supply (especially of money) when needed
    ⇒ ምስኣን
  24. Burn a hole in one's pocket = To be spent quickly
    ⇒ ምጥፋእ
  25. Feel sorry for = Pity
    ⇒ ምሕዛን
  26. Tighten one's belt = Economize (save)
    ⇒ ምቑጣብ
  27. Do with out = Live with out something
    ⇒ ዝኾነ ነገር ከይተጠቐምካ
  28. Get along = Manage (Control)
    ⇒ ምቁፅፃር
  29. Brown bag = Bring one's lunch from home
    ⇒ ምግቢ ምቑፃር
  30. In the chips = Having plenty of money
    ⇒ ሃፍታም
  31. A-1 = Excellent
    ⇒ ብጣዕሚ ፅቡቕ
  32. Set one back = Cost
    ⇒ ዋጋ
  33. An arm and a leg = A large amount of money / Expensive
    ⇒ ብዙሕ ገንዘብ / ክባር
  34. In the market for = Wanting or ready to buy
    ⇒ ንኽትገዝእ ምድላይ (ምዝግጃው)
  35. For the time being = At the present time
    ⇒ ኣብዚ ሐዚ ሰዓት
  36. Jalopy = Old car usually in poor condition
    ⇒ ኣረጊት (ከርካሳ) መኪና መ/ኡ ኣብ ፀገም እዋን
  37. In a pinch = Okay! when nothing else is available
    ⇒ እሺ! ዝኾነ ነገር እንተዘይሃልዩ
  38. Grand = 1000$
    ⇒ ክቡር / ዓብዪ / ኩሩዕ
  39. That ain't hay = That is a lot of money
    ⇒ ክባር 'ዩ / ዋጋ ኣለዎ 'ዩ
  40. Out of the blue = Unexpectedly, by surprise from no where
    ⇒ ዘይተፀበኻዮ
  41. On a shoestring = With very little money
    ⇒ ብንእሽተይ ገንዘብ
  42. A feather in one's cap = Proud achievement
    ⇒ ዘኹርዕ ስራሕ/ ውፅኢት
  43. Take a beating = Lose money
    ⇒ ምኽሳር
  44. Strike while the iron is hot = Take advantage of chance
    ⇒ ዕድልካ ምጥቃም
  45. Through the mill = Experienced in difficulties of life
    ⇒ ናይ ህይወት ዕንቕፋት / ሽግር ዝረኣየ
  46. Well heeled = Wealthy
    ⇒ ሃብቲ
  47. Wind up = End/ finish
    ⇒ ወደአ / ዛዘመ / ኣጠናቐቐ
