Phrasal verbs

Phrasal verb :- is formed by the combination of a verb and preposition.

⇒ Preposition is a word used before noun and pronoun to show direction, time, place and location.

Examples:

  1. Figure out = understand
    ⇒ ምርዳእ
  2. Work out = to solve a problem
    ⇒ ፀገም ምፍታሕ
  3. Pick up = to lift/ collect
    ⇒ ምልዓል
  4. Pick out = to select/ choose
    ⇒ ምምራፅ
  5. Hold on = to wait
    ⇒ ምፅባይ
  6. Hang on = to wait
    ⇒ ምፅባይ
  7. Hang out = to spend time with a friend somewhere
    ⇒ ግዜ ምሕላፍ/ ምዝኒናይ/ ምዝንጋዕ
  8. Hang up = to disconnect
    ⇒ ምቁራፅ
  9. Go ahead = to start/ continue
    ⇒ ጀምር/ ቀፅል
  10. Fill out = to complete a form
    ⇒ ንሓደ ቅጥዒ ምምላእ
  11. Find out = discover
    ⇒ ምድላይ/ ምርካብ
  12. Put on/ turn on = give light/ switch on
    ⇒ ምውላዕ
  13. Put off/ turn off = stop light/ switch off
    ⇒ ምጥፋእ
  14. Stand by = to support/ to wait for
    ⇒ ምሕጋዝ/ ምፅባይ/ ምዝግጃው
  15. Eat out = to eat at a restaurant
    ⇒ ኣብ ደገ(ቤት-ምግቢ) ምብላዕ
  16. Take off = start to fly/ to remove clothing
    ⇒ ምብራር(ምልዓል) ንነፋሪት/ ክዳን ምውፃእ
  17. Look after = to take care of something
    ⇒ ምሕላው/ ምክንኻን
  18. Lay off = to dismiss/ to fire
    ⇒ ምብራር/ ምስጓግ/ ምቁራፅ
  19. Carry out = to do a task
    ⇒ ስራሕ ምስራሕ
  20. Come on = to approach
    ⇒ ምምፃእ/ ምቕራብ/ ምፅጋዕ
  21. Carry out = continue
    ⇒ ቀፅል
  22. Pass away = die
    ⇒ ሞተ/ ዓረፈ
  23. Look for = try to find
    ⇒ ምድላይ
  24. Run out = nothing left
    ⇒ ምውዳእ/ ምጽንቓቅ
  25. Wake up = stop sleeping
    ⇒ ምንቃሕ/ ምብርባር
  26. Go back = return back
    ⇒ ምምላስ
  27. Give up = Stop trying
    ⇒ ተስፋ ምቑራፅ/ ኢድ ምሃብ
  28. A = B
    ⇒ ም
  29. A = B
    ⇒ ም
  30. A = B
